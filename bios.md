## Long

Alevtina (Allie) Verbovetskaya (she/her) is the University Director of Library Systems at The City University of New York (CUNY), where she leads a team of systems librarians responsible for library services platforms, discovery layers, proxy servers, and a growing portfolio of shared tools across CUNY's 31 academic libraries, 26 campuses, and more than 250,000 students. She has spent her entire career in CUNY libraries, working through reference, instruction, e-resources, technical services, and web services before moving into systems. She holds an Advanced Certificate in Project Management from CUNY School of Professional Studies, an MLIS from Rutgers University, and a BS in Computer Science from Brooklyn College.
