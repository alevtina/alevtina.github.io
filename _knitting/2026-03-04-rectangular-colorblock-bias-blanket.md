Since I’m using a self-striping yarn, I’m using the Purl Soho pattern for construction only.

Here’s the adjusted pattern:

---

Cast on 5 sts.

SECTION 1: INCREASE
Row 1 (RS): K2, M1L, knit to last 2 sts, M1R, k2. \[2 sts increased\]
Row 2 (WS): P2, knit to last 2 sts, p2.
Repeat Rows 1-2 until you have completely used 3 cakes (1,032 yards).

SECTION 2: CONTINUE
Row 1 (RS): K2, M1L, knit to last 3 sts, k2tog, k1. \[stitch count maintained\]
Row 2 (WS): P2, knit to last 2 sts, p2.
Repeat Rows 1-2 until you have completely used cakes 4, 5, and 6 (another 1,032 yards).

SECTION 3: DECREASE
Row 1 (RS): K1, ssk, knit to last 3 sts, k2tog, k1. \[2 sts decreased\]
Row 2 (WS): P2, knit to last 2 sts, p2.
Repeat Rows 1-2 until you have used cakes 7, 8, and 9 completely (another 1,032 yards).
You should end with 5 stitches.

Bind off remaining stitches.
