12/21/10 — I was hoping to be finished earlier... but there you have it.  I steam blocked it over a bowl because the decreases made it slightly pointy.  (Photo is of me at 7 AM on 12/22/10.  Not a pretty sight.)

12/19/10 — Worked two decrease rounds over 8 rounds.  Slow going >_<

12/18/10 — Joined the second color!  It's my first time working with color.  (Other than stripes, I mean.)  Quite exciting.  The effect wasn't coming out as well as I'd liked at first but I think it's getting there.  With the two strands of yarn working together, the fabric is super thick!  Very cushy.  I hope RF likes it.  (The holiday party is in 4 days.  I have no time to worry about anyone liking their gifts!)

12/17/10 — Cast on for the medium size but went up a needle size.  Knit in 1&times;1 rib for slightly more than 1.25 inches.
