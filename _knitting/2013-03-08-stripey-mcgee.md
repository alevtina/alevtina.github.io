I had some leftover worsted weight yarn in two colors so I made a striped child-sized hat:

(I used gray yarn as MC and pink yarn as CC.)


---

Using MC, cast on 76 sts using Long Tail Cast On.
Join to knit in the round.
Place marker to mark beginning of round.

Begin corrugated 2&times;2 rib pattern:
&#42;k2 in MC, p2 in CC&#42; around.
Work rib for 2.5 in.

Begin body:
Using MC, knit 8 rnds around.
Using CC, knit 8 rnds around.
Using MC, knit 8 rnds around.
Using CC, knit 2 rnds around.

Begin crown decreases (using CC for all rounds):
&#42;k6, k2tog&#42; until 4 sts remain, k4.
Knit 2 rnds around.
&#42;k5, k2tog&#42; until 4 sts remain, k4.
Knit 1 rnd around.
&#42;k4, k2tog&#42; until 4 sts remain, k4.
Knit 1 rnd around.
&#42;k3, k2tog&#42; until 4 sts remain, k4.
Knit 1 rnd around.
&#42;k2, k2tog&#42; around.
Knit 1 rnd around.
&#42;k1, k2tog&#42; around.
Knit 1 rnd around.
&#42;k2tog&#42; around.
&#42;k2tog&#42; around.
Break yarn, leaving 8 in of tail. Thread needle and run through remaining 5 sts.

Weave in ends.
Soak in Eucalan wash.
Block.
Wear!
