06/26/10 — After the swatch dried, it looks like I still need to go down a needle size.  Sigh.

06/25/10 — Swatch galore!
US 3 (3.25mm) needles: 24 sts &times; 36 rows = 4" &times; 4"
US 2 (3.0mm) needles: 26 sts &times; 38 rows = 4" &times; 4"
&uarr; That's as close as I'll get! 

06/24/10 — I don't know what I was thinking but I swatched using US 4 needles. Duh! I'm using worsted weight yarn; I need smaller needles than pattern calls for (not *larger*)! I'll make another swatch tomorrow, when this swatch dries and I'll know for **sure** that I did it wrong **:**P  **ETA**: 20 sts &times; 32 rows = 4" &times; 4" on US 4 needles
