03/29/2014 — These should have been bound off last weekend but I had to fly to a week-long conference and saved the binding off for when I returned. So here we are! Used Jeny's Surprisingly Stretchy Bind-Off and the socks are finally off the needles **:**D The Boyfriend&trade; loves 'em even though they're a teensy bit big. They're currently going through the wash and will be dried on a low setting. The intention is to have them shrink ever so slightly! Then they'll be perfect.

03/08/2014 — Finally found some time in which I could cast on and begin these socks! The Boyfriend&trade; really likes the way the yarn is knitting up. He loves the color. (I think it looks like chocolate.)

03/03/2014 — Going to make these toe-up and two-at-a-time to ensure identical socks. Using the basic structure of Time Traveler Socks but following the Syncopation Socks pattern.
