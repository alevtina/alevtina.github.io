Used Judy's magic cast-on to CO 118 sts using the solid brown ("MC"). Worked 3 rnds in St st, switched to CC yarn. Stripes in CC are varying in width (from 2 to 4 rnds) while the MC stripes are always 2 rnds wide.

I'll keep working until I've reached a desired length and I'll use the Kitchener stitch to close it up.
