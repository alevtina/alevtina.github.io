Simple pattern:

1. Using DK weight yarn and size US 5 needles, cast on 31 stitches using the long-tail cast on method.
2. Slip 1 stitch purlwise, \*knit 1, purl 1.\* Repeat between ** to the end of the row.
3. Repeat previous row until the desired length is reached.
4. Use a stretchy bind off method to cast off all stitches.
