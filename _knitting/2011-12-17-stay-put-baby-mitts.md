So tiny! So cute! So quick! I used size US 6 needles to size them down a little and create a denser fabric. Also omitted the holes above the cuffs because I won't be threading a ribbon through the mitts. I also didn't create a cord to attach the two mittens together because ... well, because I had no time >_<  This was a last minute gift, along with the [hat][1] and booties.
