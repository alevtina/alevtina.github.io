Modifications made to the pattern:

* Used smaller needles (US 6) for the top and bottom borders.
* Accidentally made the top and bottom borders a bit chunkier because I was following instructions for the medium size :zany_face:
* Slipped the first stitch purlwise of each row.

---

**2023-10-27**: Fourth ball of yarn joined.

**2023-10-22**: Eight repeats of the cable pattern complete. On the third hank of yarn.

**2023-10-20**: Making good progress! Total of 5 repeats of the cable pattern completed.

**2023-10-18**: Another repeat completed, bringing the total to 2. I learned it takes me about 2 hours to knit ~2,400 stitches.

**2023-10-17**: One repeat of the cable pattern done. It’s a little fiddly (my cable needle is large and slippery) but the pattern is memorizable. I’m just finding it a bit tedious. I hope once there’s some length on the needles, I’ll be motivated to keep going.

**2023-10-16**: Cast on for the medium size first but that seemed huge so ripped it out and cast on for the small size. Seems better. Using size US 6 needles for the top/bottom border and will switch to US 8 needles for the main portion, to try to combat the hourglass issue reported by others.
