05/22/10 — Well, that was quick!  I slightly altered the pattern, though, because that lace pattern was not working for me.  Here's what I did:

1). Cast on 63 sts on smaller needles. Worked 1&times;1 rib for 6 rows.
2). Switched to larger needles. Knit 31 sts, k2tog, k 31.  Purled one row.
3). Worked new lace pattern:

k3, &#42;k1, k2tog, yo, k1, yo, ssk, k1; repeat from &#42; until 3 sts remain, k3
k1, p until 1 st remains, k1
k3, &#42;k2tog, yo, k3, yo, ssk; repeat from &#42; until 3 sts remain, k3
k1, p until 1 st remains, k1
k3, &#42;k1, ssk, yo, k1, yo, k2tog, k1; repeat from &#42; until 3 sts remain, k3
k1, p until 1 st remains, k1

4). Worked in St st until piece was 4.5" from CO edge, ending in purl row.  Increased 1 st toward center of bonnet.  Switched to DPNs, worked crown shaping in the round.

I made 2 I-cord ties (3 sts wide, 9.5-10" long) and attached them to the front corners of the ribbing.  Such a cute hat! **:**)
