08/18/14 — Thirteen inches on the body. (Just started a third skein.) Four more to go before starting the sleeves.

08/14/14 — Sweater measurements received from my mother:
* Chest: 48"
* Length: 17" (armpit to bottom edge)
* Sleeves: 19"

My swatch with US 8 needles measured 17 sts &times; 26 rows = 4 in so I'm going for the medium size and hoping the finished product will be approximately 48-50" around the chest. Cast on 208 sts and away I go!
