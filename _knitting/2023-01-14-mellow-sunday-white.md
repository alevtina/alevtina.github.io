**2023-01-14**: One sleeve complete! I used [The Chilly Dog’s tutorial for picking up sleeve stitches](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=euTp37dlhlM) but there’s still a gap :white_frowning_face:

**2023-01-12**: Bound off the body using Jeny's Surprisingly Stretchy Bind-Off.

**2023-01-10**: Worked to 11” under arm (accidentally went a little too long) before starting 2x2 rib. Started on a third skein!

**2023-01-08**: It’s slow going but it’s going! I had my almost 5-year-old try it on (I was afraid it’d be way too big) and it seems to fit him fine! Should be fine for him when he enters kindergarten next year.

**2022-12-29**: Attempt #2 with this pattern! Used the Long Tail Cast On using the larger needles. I will be doing the short rows to lengthen the back but will use regular wrap & turn short rows. As with my previous attempts, I’m taking the front panel all the way down to the hem.
