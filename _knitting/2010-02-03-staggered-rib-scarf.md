02/04/10 — Blocked dimensions are 6.5" &times; 78".  It's soooo soft.  I'm tempted to keep it for myself!

02/03/10 — Used up two hanks of the yarn to finish this scarf. Final [unblocked] measurements are 6.5"&times;66". I will soak it in a wool wash now and see what happens tomorrow **;**)

01/29/10 — I cast on 28 stitches to make a wider scarf.  And I'm in love with this yarn! It's so soft and luxurious. Very drapey, too. However, I'm not sure how I feel about the first slipped stitch of every row. It's supposed to make the edges look neat but they look sloppy instead. I guess we'll see what happens when I'm finished with the item.
